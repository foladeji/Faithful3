'An Eye for Dialogue'

This concept employs google glasses a means for augmenting theatrical experriences for the deaf and hearing impair , designed to provide them closed captions for the performance they're viewing, in real time.

The design of the captioning interface was informed by several factors in order to produce an optimal experience, including the continuity of conversation and the limited human ability to visually track simultaneous conversations of varying significance occuring in real time.
